Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds are provided for deposits, contest entries, and other transactions on Tarang Win.

1. Deposit Refunds

Successful Deposits

  • Once a deposit is successfully credited to your wallet, it cannot be refunded to your original payment method
  • Deposited funds can only be used to participate in contests or withdrawn according to our withdrawal policy

Failed Deposits

  • If your payment is deducted but not credited to your wallet, the amount will be automatically refunded within 5-7 working days
  • This refund is processed by your bank/payment gateway, not by Tarang Win
  • If the refund is not received after 7 days, please contact support with your transaction details

Double Deduction

  • If you are charged twice for a single deposit, the excess amount will be refunded within 7-10 working days
  • Please share both transaction IDs with support for faster processing

2. Contest Entry Refunds

No Refund Policy

  • Once you join a contest, the entry fee is non-refundable
  • This applies regardless of whether you participate in the contest or not
  • No refunds are provided for poor performance, disconnection, or device issues during the contest

Contest Cancellation

  • If Tarang Win cancels a contest before it starts, 100% of the entry fee will be refunded to your deposit wallet
  • If a contest is cancelled after it starts due to technical issues, refunds will be decided on a case-by-case basis
  • Refunds for cancelled contests are processed within 24-48 hours

Technical Issues

  • If you experience technical issues preventing you from participating, you may request a refund
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of the contest
  • Technical issues will be verified from our server logs before processing refunds
  • Internet connectivity issues on your end do not qualify for refunds

3. Withdrawal Refunds

  • Once a withdrawal is approved and processed, it cannot be reversed or refunded to your wallet
  • If you entered incorrect UPI details, contact support immediately before the withdrawal is processed
  • If money is sent to an incorrect UPI ID due to user error, Tarang Win is not responsible for the loss
  • If a withdrawal fails due to technical issues, the amount will be returned to your winning wallet

4. Bonus Wallet Refunds

  • Bonus wallet funds (referral bonuses, promotional rewards) are non-refundable
  • Bonus funds cannot be withdrawn directly and must be used for contest entries
  • If your account is terminated for violating terms, bonus funds will be forfeited
  • Expired bonuses cannot be reinstated or refunded

5. Refund Request Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact support at tsp.chainpura@gmail.com
  2. Provide your registered mobile number
  3. Include transaction ID or contest ID
  4. Describe the issue in detail
  5. Attach any relevant screenshots (payment receipt, error messages)

Processing Time

  • Refund requests are reviewed within 3-5 working days
  • Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 working days
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

6. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following situations:

  • Voluntary decision not to participate after joining a contest
  • Poor performance or low score in a contest
  • Internet disconnection or device issues during contest
  • Forgetting to participate in a joined contest
  • Change of mind after depositing money
  • Account closure or deletion (wallet balance is forfeited)
  • Violation of terms and conditions leading to account suspension
  • Incorrect UPI details provided by user for withdrawal

7. Dispute Resolution

  • If your refund request is denied, you can appeal by providing additional evidence
  • All refund decisions are final and binding
  • In case of disputes, the decision of Tarang Win management will be final
  • For legal disputes, the matter will be subject to Indian courts' jurisdiction

8. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through the app and website. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact for Refunds

For refund-related queries, contact us at:

  • Email: tsp.chainpura@gmail.com
  • Subject: Refund Request - [Your Mobile Number]
  • Response Time: Within 3-5 working days
