Responsible Gaming

Our commitment to safe and responsible gaming

Tarang Win is committed to promoting responsible gaming practices. We believe that skill-based gaming should be an enjoyable entertainment activity, not a source of financial or personal problems. This policy outlines our commitment to responsible gaming and the tools we provide to help you game responsibly.

1. Our Commitment

Tarang Win is dedicated to:

  • Providing a safe and fair gaming environment
  • Protecting vulnerable players from gaming-related harm
  • Promoting awareness about responsible gaming
  • Implementing measures to prevent underage gaming
  • Offering tools for players to control their gaming activity
  • Providing support for players who may need help

2. Age Verification

  • Tarang Win is strictly for users aged 18 and above
  • We verify age during registration through mobile OTP authentication
  • We reserve the right to request additional age verification documents
  • Accounts found to belong to minors will be immediately terminated
  • Any deposits made by minors will be refunded to the payment source

3. Responsible Gaming Tools

Deposit Limits

  • Players can set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits
  • These limits help control spending and prevent excessive deposits
  • Once a limit is reached, further deposits are blocked until the period resets
  • Decreasing limits takes effect immediately
  • Increasing limits has a 24-hour cooling-off period

Self-Exclusion

  • Players can request temporary self-exclusion from the platform
  • Exclusion periods available: 24 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or permanent
  • During exclusion, you cannot join contests or make deposits
  • Permanent exclusion can only be reversed after 6 months with a written request

Time Limits

  • Players can set daily time limits for app usage
  • Notifications remind you when your time limit is approaching
  • Once the limit is reached, the app will restrict further participation

Account Closure

  • Players can request permanent account closure at any time
  • Closure requests are processed within 24 hours
  • Remaining wallet balance will be forfeited upon closure

4. Warning Signs of Problem Gaming

Be aware of these warning signs that may indicate gaming is becoming a problem:

  • Spending more money or time on gaming than you can afford
  • Neglecting work, studies, or personal relationships due to gaming
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gaming
  • Feeling anxious, irritable, or restless when not gaming
  • Lying to family or friends about your gaming activity
  • Chasing losses by spending more to win back money
  • Gaming to escape problems or negative feelings
  • Failed attempts to control or stop gaming

If you experience any of these signs, we strongly recommend using our responsible gaming tools or seeking professional help.

5. Tips for Responsible Gaming

  • Set a Budget: Only deposit what you can afford to lose
  • Time Management: Set limits on your gaming time
  • Take Breaks: Take regular breaks from gaming
  • Balance: Maintain balance with other activities and responsibilities
  • Don't Chase Losses: Accept losses as part of the game
  • Play for Fun: View gaming as entertainment, not income
  • Stay Informed: Educate yourself about responsible gaming
  • Seek Help: Don't hesitate to ask for help if needed
