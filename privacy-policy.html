Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Tarang Win ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name: Full name provided during registration
  • Mobile Number: Used for OTP authentication and communication
  • Email Address: Optional, for account recovery and updates
  • UPI ID: For withdrawal processing
  • PAN Number: Required for withdrawals above ₹10,000 for TDS compliance

Financial Information

  • Transaction history (deposits, withdrawals, contest entries)
  • Wallet balances (deposit, winning, bonus wallets)
  • Payment method details (processed securely via Cashfree)

Usage Information

  • Contest participation history
  • Quiz performance and scores
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • App usage patterns and preferences

Device Information

  • Device model and operating system
  • Unique device identifier
  • IP address
  • App version and crash reports

2. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: When you register, update your profile, or participate in contests
  • Automatically: When you use the app, we collect usage and device information
  • Third parties: Payment processors (Cashfree) for transaction processing
  • Firebase: For authentication, analytics, and crash reporting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account
  • Authentication: To verify your identity via OTP
  • Contest Participation: To allow you to join and participate in contests
  • Payment Processing: To process deposits and withdrawals
  • TDS Compliance: To calculate and deduct TDS as per government regulations
  • Leaderboard: To display your ranking and performance
  • Communication: To send contest notifications, results, and updates
  • Security: To detect fraud and protect our platform
  • Analytics: To improve our services and user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Cashfree for payment processing
  • Firebase: For authentication, analytics, and cloud services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or government authorities
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Firebase Security Rules for database protection
  • Secure payment gateway integration (Cashfree)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data within our organization

6. Data Retention

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Transaction History: Retained for 7 years as per tax regulations
  • Usage Data: Retained for 2 years for analytics and improvement
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion request, except where legally required to retain

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us at tsp.chainpura@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

Tarang Win is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and sending a notification through the app. Your continued use of the service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Address: India
