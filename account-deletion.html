Account Deletion

Learn how to permanently delete your account

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this policy carefully before proceeding.

1. What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated within 24-48 hours
  • You will no longer be able to log in or access the app
  • All your personal data will be deleted from our servers
  • You will be removed from all active contests
  • Any pending withdrawals will be cancelled
  • All wallet balances will be forfeited
  • You will lose all referral bonuses and rewards
  • Your transaction history will be deleted
  • You will be unsubscribed from all notifications

2. Data Retention

While most data is deleted, some information may be retained:

  • Financial Records: Transaction data may be retained for 7 years as per tax regulations
  • Audit Logs: Account activity logs may be retained for security and audit purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be retained if required by law or legal proceedings
  • Fraud Prevention: Device and IP information may be retained to prevent fraud

All retained data is stored securely and used only for the purposes stated above.

3. Before You Delete Your Account

Please complete the following before requesting deletion:

  • Withdraw Your Balance: Withdraw all money from your wallet before deletion
  • Complete Active Contests: Finish any ongoing contests you're participating in
  • Clear Pending Withdrawals: Ensure no withdrawal requests are pending
  • Download Data: If you want your data, request it before deletion
  • Use Bonuses: Use any bonus wallet balance as it will be forfeited

4. How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Through the App

  1. Open Tarang Win app
  2. Go to Profile section
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. Confirm your decision by entering your mobile number
  7. Submit the deletion request

Method 2: Via Email

  1. Send an email to tsp.chainpura@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Mobile Number]"
  3. Include your registered mobile number in the body
  4. Confirm that you understand the consequences
  5. We will process your request within 24-48 hours

5. Account Deletion Request Form

When requesting deletion, please provide:

  • Registered mobile number
  • Full name (as per registration)
  • Reason for deletion (optional but helpful)
  • Confirmation that you understand the consequences
  • Confirmation that you have withdrawn all funds

6. Processing Time

  • Deletion requests are processed within 24-48 hours
  • You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete
  • You will not be able to access your account after this confirmation
  • If you change your mind, contact us immediately before processing is complete

7. What Cannot Be Recovered

After account deletion, the following cannot be recovered:

  • Account access and login credentials
  • Wallet balances (deposit, winning, and bonus)
  • Contest history and participation records
  • Leaderboard rankings and achievements
  • Referral bonuses and rewards
  • Transaction history
  • Personal data and profile information

8. Re-Registration

  • You may register again with the same mobile number after account deletion
  • This will be treated as a new account with no history
  • Previous balances, rewards, and data cannot be restored
  • You will need to complete the registration process again
  • Any previous bonuses or promotions will not apply to the new account

9. Alternative Options

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

Self-Exclusion

  • If you want to take a break, use our self-exclusion feature
  • You can exclude yourself for 24 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or permanently
  • Your account and data remain intact during exclusion
  • You can return after the exclusion period ends

Unsubscribe from Notifications

  • If notifications are bothersome, unsubscribe from them
  • You can still use the app without receiving notifications

Contact Support

  • If you're facing issues, contact our support team
  • We may be able to resolve your concern without account deletion

10. Data Export Request

If you want your data before deletion:

  • Send an email to tsp.chainpura@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Data Export Request - [Your Mobile Number]"
  • We will provide your data within 7-10 working days
  • Data will be provided in a readable format (JSON or CSV)
  • Request data export before submitting deletion request

11. Contact for Account Deletion

To request account deletion or if you have questions:

  • Email: tsp.chainpura@gmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - [Your Mobile Number]
  • Processing Time: 24-48 hours
