Skill Game Declaration

Official declaration regarding the nature of Tarang Win

This declaration clarifies that Tarang Win is a skill-based gaming platform and not a gambling or betting platform. All contests on Tarang Win are designed to test players' knowledge, speed, and accuracy.

1. Declaration

Tarang Win hereby declares that:

  • Tarang Win is a skill-based gaming platform
  • All contests offered on Tarang Win are games of skill, not chance
  • The outcome of every contest is determined by the player's knowledge, speed, and accuracy
  • There is no element of luck, chance, or randomization in determining winners
  • Tarang Win operates in compliance with applicable laws regarding skill-based gaming in India

2. What is a Game of Skill?

A game of skill is defined as a game where the outcome is determined primarily by the player's knowledge, skill, judgment, dexterity, or adroitness, rather than by chance or luck. Key characteristics include:

  • Knowledge: Players must possess knowledge in specific subject areas
  • Practice: Performance improves with practice and experience
  • Strategy: Players can develop strategies to improve performance
  • Control: Players have control over the outcome through their actions
  • Prediction: Skilled players can consistently predict outcomes better than unskilled players

3. Why Tarang Win is a Game of Skill

Knowledge-Based Questions

  • All quiz questions are based on general knowledge, academics, or specific subjects
  • Players need knowledge to answer questions correctly
  • Knowledge can be acquired through study and experience

Time Pressure

  • Each question has a time limit
  • Players must answer quickly to maximize their score
  • Speed improves with practice and familiarity

Scoring System

  • Points are awarded for correct answers
  • No negative marking for wrong answers
  • Higher scores require more knowledge and faster response

Winner Selection

  • Winners are determined by: 1) Highest score, 2) Fastest completion time, 3) Earliest submission
  • No random draws or lottery systems
  • Server-side processing ensures fair and transparent ranking

No Element of Chance

  • No dice, cards, or random number generators
  • No hidden information or luck-based mechanics
  • Same questions for all participants in a contest
  • Question order and option order are randomized for fairness, not chance

4. Legal Position in India

The Supreme Court of India has recognized that games of skill are not gambling and are protected under Article 19(1)(g) of the Constitution. Key legal principles:

  • Games of skill are exempt from gambling laws in most Indian states
  • The distinction between skill and chance is based on the dominant factor test
  • If skill is the dominant factor in determining the outcome, it is a game of skill
  • Tarang Win operates only in states where skill-based gaming is legal
  • Players from states where skill-based gaming is prohibited cannot participate
