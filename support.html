Quick Help

📱 OTP Not Received?

Check if your mobile number is correct. Wait 2-3 minutes for the OTP. If still not received, try again after 5 minutes.

💳 Payment Failed?

Check your bank account for deduction. If deducted, wait 24 hours for auto-refund. Contact support if issue persists.

💸 Withdrawal Pending?

Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours after admin approval. Check your withdrawal status in the app.

🎮 Can't Join Contest?

Ensure you have sufficient wallet balance and the contest hasn't started. Check your internet connection.

Common Issues & Solutions

App not opening or crashing

Solution: Clear app cache, restart your phone, or reinstall the app. Ensure you have the latest version.

Money deducted but not added to wallet

Solution: Wait 24 hours for automatic processing. If not credited, share transaction details with support.

Quiz timer not working

Solution: Check your internet connection. Close and reopen the quiz. Ensure your device time is correct.

Leaderboard not updating

Solution: Refresh the page or app. Leaderboard updates in real-time but may have slight delays.
